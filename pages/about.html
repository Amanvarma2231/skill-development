About TechSkill NGO

Empowering the next generation with technical education and digital safety

Home / About Us

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive technical education and cybersecurity awareness to students in schools and colleges, bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world digital challenges.

Our Vision

To create a digitally literate generation equipped with technical skills, cybersecurity knowledge, and online safety awareness to thrive in the modern technological landscape.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to making a significant impact

2018

Foundation Established

TechSkill NGO was founded by a group of IT professionals and educators passionate about bridging the digital skills gap.

2019

First Workshop Series

Conducted our first cybersecurity awareness workshops in 10 schools, reaching over 500 students.

2020

Digital Expansion

Launched online learning platform during pandemic, expanding reach to rural areas and remote schools.

2021

Girls Safety Initiative

Started specialized programs for girls' online safety and cybersecurity, partnering with women-led tech organizations.

2022

College Partnerships

Expanded to B.Tech CSE programs in 15 colleges, offering advanced technical workshops and industry exposure.

2023

National Recognition

Received National Education Excellence Award for outstanding contribution to technical education and digital literacy.
