Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in our courses through our website by visiting the Courses page, selecting your preferred program, and clicking the "Enroll Now" button. For assistance, contact our admissions team.

Are the courses free?

Many of our basic courses are offered free of charge, while advanced programs may have nominal fees to cover resources and faculty support. We also offer scholarships and financial aid for eligible students.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our courses. Some advanced programs also offer industry-recognized certifications in partnership with tech organizations.

Can schools partner with your NGO?

Absolutely! We actively partner with schools and colleges to bring technical education programs to their students. Contact our partnerships team to discuss collaboration opportunities.

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers in various roles including teaching, mentoring, event organization, and technical support. Visit our volunteer page or contact the volunteer team to get started.

Do you offer online courses?

Yes, we offer both online and in-person courses. Our online programs include live sessions, recorded lectures, and interactive assignments to ensure comprehensive learning.
